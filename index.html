About

I study how to map neural time‑series to language semantics. My current project, MEG2SEM, aligns MEG recordings with sentence and word embeddings (E5/SONAR/ADA) and evaluates retrieval and representational geometry. I care about transfer from MEG prototypes to practical EEG/OPM systems.

Previously: published author and editor; now exploring AI‑assisted communication and accessibility.
