About

I’m interested in the general principles of intelligence and cognition. At Oxford’s PNPL lab, I study how AI can decode meaning from non‑invasive brain signals (MEG/EEG/OPM/fMRI). I build representation‑learning pipelines that map neural activity to semantic spaces and evaluate them with RSA, CKA, and retrieval metrics.
