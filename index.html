About me

I’m a researcher working at the intersection of AI and neuroscience. My work focuses on building non‑invasive brain‑to‑text systems by aligning neural signals (MEG/EEG/OPM) to large‑scale language representations. I’m part of the PNPL at Oxford, supervised by Oiwi Parker‑Jones. Broadly, I’m interested in how semantic information is represented in the brain and how we can harness that knowledge to design accessible, robust BCIs.
